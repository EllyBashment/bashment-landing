Core Features

Everything You Need
For the Night

Built for people who live in the moment — not people who spend 45 minutes in a group chat deciding.

Live Heat Map

See nightlife density across your city in real time. New to town? Red means packed, blue means hidden gem. No local knowledge required.

Vibe Score

A live 0–100 score recalculated every minute from check-ins, capacity, crowd reactions, and Vibe Check posts. The crowd is the review.

Vibe Check

10-second clips from inside the venue posted by promoters and patrons. No filters. Hear the music, see the crowd, decide instantly.

Circles & Pings

Friends ping events, you see who's going. No coordination overhead — one tap tells your circle you're thinking Lotus tonight.

In-App Navigation

Get directions to any event without leaving the app. Live route tracking, ETA, walking or driving modes — door to dance floor in one tap.

Crowd Timeline

See predicted crowd density hour by hour. Know it peaks at 11PM before you leave — or plan around it entirely.
